About DAMP Smart Drinkware

Revolutionizing beverage tracking with innovative BLE technology to ensure you never leave your drink behind again.

Our Mission

At DAMP Smart Drinkware, we're solving a universal problem that affects millions of people daily - drink abandonment. Our mission is to create intelligent, user-friendly solutions that seamlessly integrate into your daily routine while ensuring your favorite beverages never get left behind.

We believe that technology should enhance, not complicate, your life. That's why we've developed the DAMP (Drink Abandonment Monitoring Protocol) system - a revolutionary approach to beverage tracking that's both powerful and intuitive.

The Problem We Solve

Studies show that the average person abandons their drink 3-4 times per week, leading to:

  • Wasted beverages and money
  • Missed caffeine or hydration goals
  • Frustration and inconvenience
  • Environmental impact from discarded drinks

Our smart drinkware collection uses advanced BLE technology to create a connected ecosystem that keeps you aware of your beverage location at all times.
