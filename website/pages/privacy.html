Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Account preferences and settings

Device and Usage Information

From your DAMP smart drinkware devices and our app, we may collect:

  • Device usage patterns and location data (when permitted)
  • App usage statistics and preferences
  • Technical information about your device and connectivity
  • Beverage consumption patterns (anonymized)

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics and other tools to collect:

  • Website usage patterns and navigation data
  • Browser and device information
  • Geographic location (country/region level)
  • Referral sources and marketing campaign effectiveness

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our services: Process orders, deliver products, and provide customer support
  • Improve functionality: Enhance device performance and app features
  • Personalize experience: Customize app settings and recommendations
  • Communicate with you: Send order updates, product information, and support messages
  • Marketing (with consent): Share product updates and promotional offers
  • Analytics and research: Understand usage patterns to improve our products

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these situations:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services:

  • Payment processing: Stripe for secure payment handling
  • Shipping: Delivery partners for order fulfillment
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for website optimization
  • Customer support: Support platforms for assistance

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse of our services

4. Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure storage: Personal data is stored in secure, access-controlled environments
  • Regular audits: We conduct regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data
  • Privacy by design: Our products are built with privacy protection as a core feature

5. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct or update your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Device Controls

Through our app, you can:

  • Control location sharing and tracking
  • Adjust notification preferences
  • Manage connected devices
  • View and delete usage history

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings and opt out of analytics tracking.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods based on:

  • Account data: While your account is active and for 2 years after closure
  • Transaction data: For 7 years as required by financial regulations
  • Usage analytics: In anonymized form for product improvement
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers
  • Adherence to international privacy frameworks
  • Regular compliance assessments

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. When we make changes:

  • We'll post the updated policy on our website
  • We'll notify you via email for significant changes
  • The "Last Updated" date will reflect when changes were made
