Frequently Asked Questions

When will my pre-order ship? +

Current estimated shipping dates are:

  • DAMP Handle v1.0: Q3 2025
  • Silicone Bottom & Cup Sleeve: Q4 2025
  • Baby Bottle: Q1 2026

We'll send regular development updates and notify you as soon as your order ships.

How long does the battery last? +

Battery life varies by product:

  • DAMP Handle: Up to 6 months with typical use
  • Other products: 3-4 weeks with regular use

All devices use USB-C charging and include low-battery notifications in the app.

Are the products waterproof? +

Yes! All DAMP products feature IP67 water resistance, meaning they can handle:

  • Spills and splashes
  • Hand washing under running water
  • Rain and outdoor use
  • Dishwasher safe (where applicable)
Which devices are compatible? +

DAMP products work with:

  • iOS: iPhone 8 and newer (iOS 13+)
  • Android: Devices with Android 8.0+ and Bluetooth 5.0
  • Range: Up to 100 feet in open areas

Our app will be available on both the App Store and Google Play.

Can I cancel my pre-order? +

Yes, you can cancel your pre-order anytime before shipping for a full refund. We also offer:

  • Full refund guarantee if we can't deliver as promised
  • 30-day return policy after delivery
  • No-questions-asked cancellation policy

Contact support@dampdrink.com to cancel or modify your order.

How do smart zones work? +

Smart zones are locations where you've approved leaving your drink:

  • Home zone: Automatically detected from your frequent locations
  • Work zone: Set your office or workplace
  • Custom zones: Add coffee shops, gyms, or other regular locations

The app learns your patterns and reduces false alerts in approved areas.
