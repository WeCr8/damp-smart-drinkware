Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. This includes:

  • Device usage data from your DAMP smart drinkware
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Account information (email, preferences)
  • Device location data (only when using proximity features)
  • Temperature and consumption analytics

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us:

  • Provide and improve our smart drinkware services
  • Send drink abandonment alerts and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance device performance
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Develop new features and product improvements
