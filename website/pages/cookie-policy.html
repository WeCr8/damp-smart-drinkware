What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Keeping you signed in to your account
  • Analyzing how you use our website
  • Personalizing content and advertisements
  • Ensuring website security and preventing fraud

Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your cookie preferences:

  • Manage Preferences: Use our cookie settings to enable/disable cookie categories

  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting website functionality
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Data Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
